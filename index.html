How To Get Started

A simple path to a stronger ministry

You shouldn't have to wade through complexity to take the next step. Here's how it works:

1

Know the questions

Start by understanding what your students are actually wrestling with — not what we assume they're asking. Our question events surface the real concerns, anonymously and honestly, so you can lead from reality instead of guesswork.

2

Engage with students

Meet students where they are with thoughtful, honest conversation. Whether through a facilitated event or your own equipped leaders, students learn that their questions are welcome — and that the church has something real to say.

3

Deepen your relationships

The questions are the doorway, not the destination. As trust builds, conversations move from one-off Q&A to ongoing discipleship — and your leaders become the people students keep coming back to.
